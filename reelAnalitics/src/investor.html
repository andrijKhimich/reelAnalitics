Reel Analytics is a sports technology and data company that analyzes video to measure athleticism for coaches to evaluate players more accurately.

Although the modern-day athlete is bigger and more athletic than ever before, there hasn't been a change in the way athleticism is measured since the early 1900s. As a result, the same tools used to measure athleticism in the 1900s yielded poor player evaluation results today. 67% of players recruited or drafted by teams are not evaluated accurately, resulting in an 18 billion dollar loss in salary, scholarships, and financial aid annually. In fact, more than 70% of NFL rookies will not sign a second contract.

At Reel Analytics we’ve solved this problem by moving beyond the stopwatch, the tape measure and the scale using our technology to unlock new athletic data from video.

With our position-specific in-game athleticism metrics, teams can more accurately evaluate the athletic ability of players they're recruiting.
