Reel Analytics: Delivering Athletic Data from Video.

Reel Analytics is a sports technology and data company that analyzes video to measure athleticism for coaches to evaluate players more accurately.

IGA Score.

Using position-specific metrics extracted directly from prospects' film, we created the In-Game Athleticism (IGA) Score to measure players' athleticism in the context of a game.

We trained our scoring model by measuring the in-game athleticism of more than 15,000 college and NFL players for current and historical context.

Our IGA Scores have been used by several FBS and FCS programs, including the University of Georgia, University of Michigan, and Jackson State University to verify the athletic ability of high school and transfer portal prospects and players on their roster.
